# K Kumar

K Kumar is an Indian film producer known for his work across Hindi, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu, Gujarati, and Bhojpuri cinema. He has produced several commercially successful films and music videos spanning regional and mainstream entertainment markets.

## Selected Notable Films
- Vrusshbha
- Fuffad Ji
- Garudan
- Single Shankarum Smartphone Simranum
- Paris Jeyaraj
- Maaman

## K Kumar Studios
K Kumar is the founder of **K Kumar Studios**, a Mumbai-based production company engaged in the development and production of films, web content, and music-driven projects within the Indian entertainment industry.

## Music Projects
In the music domain, K Kumar has produced multiple music videos across independent and mainstream platforms. Among these, he has worked as a **co-producer** on the Hindi hip-hop track *Trap Munde* by Ikka and Badshah, and as a **producer** on *Sab Gazab* by Goldkartz and Badshah.

## Upcoming Projects
Several films, web series, and music projects are currently in development.
